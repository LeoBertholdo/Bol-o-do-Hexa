- **+3 and -2 get the full beats**; **+1 and 0** appear only as chips in the closing recap (no room for their own beats in 8s). If you'd rather flash all four as quick beats, say so and I'll re-time it.

- The example uses a consistent pick of **2–1** so the outcomes contrast cleanly: 2–1 (cravada +3) vs 1–2 (invertido -2), with 3–0 (+1) and 0–2 (0) shown in the recap.
